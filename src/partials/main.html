Mall Blitz

Game Overview

Mall Blitz is a fast-paced, fun-filled shopping adventure set in a quirky mall packed with mini-games, surprises, and challenges. Play as energetic shoppers rushing through stores, grabbing deals, dodging chaos, and competing in fun challenges. Whether you’re into speed, memory games, or quick puzzles — Mall Blitz turns shopping into an exciting arcade experience!

Core Features

Mini-Games Galore

Each shop is its own challenge: puzzles, reflex games, time trials, and more.

Wacky Shoppers

Play as different characters, each with their own shopping style.

Mall Mayhem Mode

Time-limited challenges that test your skills under pressure.

Unlockable Shops

From fashion boutiques to arcade halls — every level is unique.

Power-Ups & Surprises

Use gadgets to outsmart the crowd and beat the clock.

How it works

  • Choose your shopper character

  • Complete mini-games across different mall zones

  • Use collected items and bonuses to unlock new stores

  • Beat levels to earn coins, upgrades, and surprise rewards

  • Aim for the high score and become the mall’s top shopper!
